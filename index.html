Maintenance & research tools

Use these to search TXT, verify catalog links, locate .txt files across repos, and relocate files + refresh catalogs in Law-Texts-ui.

Search

Texts Search Tool

Query across Textbooks (catalog), Laws, and Rules on texts.wwwbcb.org. Shows quotable snippets and links to full TXT.

 Catalogs

Catalog link checker

Checks every url_txt in texts/catalog.json, /laws.json and /rules.json for 404s.

 Locator

.txt Locator — textbooks + laws + rules

Scan across repos to find normalized .txt files and see their canonical destinations: Law-Texts-ui/data/textbooks/<jersey|uk>/, /data/laws/<jersey|uk>/, /data/rules/<jersey|uk>/.

Curate

Relocate TXT & refresh catalogs

Opens a PR to move files into canonical folders in Law-Texts-ui and updates texts, laws, and rules catalogs. Requires configured token.

 Diagnostics

Simple fetch check

Give a URL; fetches and shows status/body to help debug path or CORS issues.
